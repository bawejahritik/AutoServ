Our Services

Interim

An interim service includes around 50 detailed checks

  • oil change
  • oil filter replacement
  • check steering, brakes, clutch, tyres, hoses and pipes.
  • check levels of fluids(brake, antifreeze coolant)
  • screen wash
  • check condition of engine (such as plug and filter)

Detailed reports of each check.

Full

It includes all basic interim service checks plus 15-20 more.

  • Suspension/steering check
  • Exterior lights operation
  • check Underbonnet (report leaks)
  • check Radiator condition/leaks
  • pollen filter condition

Detailed reports of each check and recommendation for further care.

Major

It contains all services (interim + full) and detailed check and replacements.

  • visual check of DPF system and Report(diesel models)
  • replace fiel filter
  • check and top up transfer box and differential oil.
  • replace spark plugs
  • Grease streeing and suspension joints
  • check and report DPF fluid level
  • check and top up gearbox oil

Personal assistance and recommendations about future care
